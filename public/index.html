A wider toe box

Runners told designer ther wanted more room in the forefoot, so Nike added width, making the shoe more comfortable

Offset laces

Nike moved the laces toward the outside of the shoe to mitigate extra pressure and friction on the top of the foot when runners cinch the laces down.

Heel fit

Designers nailed the heel fit by adding a small cushion inside the shoe that wraps around the Achilles.

SPECIFICATIONS

  • The new shoe feels bouncier because there’s 15 percent more ZoomX foam in the midsole.
  • There’s also more rubber in the forefoot to improve grip on wet roads.
  • The new upper material is lighter and less water-absorbent than Flyknit.
  • The plate in the shoe is made of carbon fiber, so you can have more impulse with each step
  • Weight: 6.6 Oz (men's 9 size)
